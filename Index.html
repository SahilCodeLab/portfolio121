My skills

My creative skills & experiences.

In 2021, I developed an interest in editing and began learning it on my phone. Between school and small jobs, I dedicated time to improve my skills. Slowly, I started seeing progress in my work and kept learning new techniques. Today, I put my full effort into everything I do, always striving to be better. Editing has become more than just a hobby for me; it’s a journey where each new challenge helps me grow and improve.

My Journey into Graphic Design From 2021 to 2022, I was focused on editing, but during this time, I discovered graphic design. My interest grew even more as I explored what it was, how it worked, and what was required to succeed in it. Since graphic design was similar to editing and built upon the skills I already had, I shifted my focus towards it. I created many projects, shared them online, and received a lot of love and appreciation from others. This made me realize that graphic design was a great path to choose, and it felt like I was on the right track.

My Journey in Creating Portfolio Websites I was working as a graphic designer, but I wasn’t getting much exposure. I decided to create a website to showcase my projects and learned that it's called a portfolio website. I had no idea how to make one, so I researched and found that it involves languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I learned HTML in about a month, CSS took me 3 months, and JavaScript took around 6 months. I then created my first project on GitHub. My first portfolio website took 7 months to complete, but it didn’t get the response I wanted. By the end of 2024, I created 6 websites, 3 of them being portfolios, but none of them worked out well.

Finally, in just 3 days, I built my final portfolio website, which I am really proud of.
